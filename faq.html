Working together

What does Expert Effect actually do?

We provide human resources, organizational effectiveness, and stress-resiliency support through a vetted network of 100+ seasoned specialists. Instead of a single generalist, you are matched with the exact expert your situation calls for, whether that is a handbook rewrite, a culture change initiative, or performance coaching for a high achiever.

Who do you work with?

Three groups: small businesses that want top-notch HR without a full in-house department, mid to large companies that need to hand off a project or an entire HR function, and high performers such as athletes, executives, celebrities, and students who need to hold up under relentless pressure.

How is this different from a typical HR firm?

Most firms keep you at arm's length with a third-party, one-size-fits-all approach. We become an integral partner. Every engagement is fully customized and delivered with genuine relational care, by a specialist with 12 to 25+ years in that exact field.

How do we get started?

Tell us what you are facing through the contact form or a quick call. We map out the challenge, match you with the right expert, and share a plan built around your reality, no cookie-cutter proposals.

Services & scope

What areas of HR do you cover?

Compliance and handbooks, onboarding and records, talent acquisition, performance management, benefits and leaves, employee relations, compensation and equity, change management, investigations, HRIS implementation, compliance audits, and more. For larger teams we also mentor in-house HR staff and run full compliance audits.

Do you offer interim or fractional HR support?

Yes. For a leave, a surge in work, or an open role, we drop in a seasoned HR professional to hold the line, on-site or virtual, for as long as you need.

What is stress-resiliency, and who is it for?

It is coaching for the people the pressure never lets up on. We train body and mind, drawing on strength and conditioning, clinical nutrition, neuropsychology, and mental-performance expertise, including Olympic-level experience, to build performance readiness and prevent burnout.

Do you provide training and workshops?

Yes. Expert-designed, high-caliber workshops delivered in-person or virtually, covering everything from harassment prevention and HR fundamentals for supervisors to emotional agility, leadership development, and culture transformation.

Logistics & pricing

Are services in-person or virtual?

Both. We offer in-person support across Los Angeles, San Diego, San Francisco, Fresno, Las Vegas, Phoenix, Denver, and Miami, plus fully virtual support throughout the United States. We are expanding to Dallas, New York, Houston, and Seattle between 2026 and 2028.

How does pricing work?

It depends on the engagement. Small businesses typically work with us on a reasonable monthly retainer for ongoing HR support. Larger companies engage us per project or per function. Resiliency and coaching are scoped to the individual. We share clear pricing after a short conversation about your needs.

How are your experts vetted?

Every expert is a highly vetted, seasoned professional with deep tenure in their exact field. Our network includes specialists who have led work at organizations such as NASA JPL, the U.S. Air Force, Virgin Galactic, Terran Orbital, and Olympic programs.

How quickly can we be matched with an expert?

Usually within a few business days of our first conversation. Because we draw from a deep bench rather than a single consultant's calendar, we can move quickly to put the right specialist on your challenge.
